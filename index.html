Common Questions

How does the app calculate when I need to leave?
OnCue Go uses your destination, travel mode, and buffer time to calculate your departure schedule. It monitors route traffic changes in the background and warns you if your departure window shifts.
What do the points and streaks mean?
Points keep you motivated! You earn points for leaving/arriving early or on time, and lose points for no-shows. Build your punctuality streak by staying consistent.
Why is the app optimized for ADHD?
People with ADHD often experience time blindness. This app is designed to trigger timely alerts, sound alarms, and display Live Activities on your lock screen so you don't lose track of time.
Is my location data secure?
Absolutely. All your routing, schedules, and location coordinates are processed on your device and are never shared or sent to external database servers.
How do I reset my streaks or progress?
You can manage your user profiles, review points breakdown, or reset your streaks directly from the Settings tab in the app.
