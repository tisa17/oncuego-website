Common Questions

How does amILate? calculate when I need to leave?
amILate? uses your destination, travel mode, and any buffer time you set to work backwards from your arrival time — giving you a precise moment to walk out the door.
What do the points mean?
Points are a way to track your punctuality over time. You earn points for arriving early or on time, and lose points for late arrivals or no-shows. Your total score reflects your overall consistency — think of it as a personal punctuality streak.
Why is the app designed for ADHD?
Time blindness — difficulty sensing how much time is passing — is a core challenge for many people with ADHD. amILate? removes the mental math of departure planning so your brain can focus on actually getting ready, not calculating.
Can I set a buffer for getting ready?
Yes! When creating a trip, you can add extra buffer time to account for getting dressed, finding your keys, or any other pre-departure routine that takes time.
My points seem off — what should I do?
If your points look incorrect, try closing and reopening the app — it will automatically repair any outdated trip records. If the issue persists, contact us and we'll sort it out.
Is my data stored on my device?
Yes. All your trips and progress data are stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit personal data to any external servers.
